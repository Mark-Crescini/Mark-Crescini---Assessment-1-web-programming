Steak Recipe

Description:
This steak recipe is a quick guide on how to cook a good steak. This recipe doesn't require a lot of ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Steak
    • Sirloin
    • Ribeye (Recommended)
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Cooking oil
    • Avocado Oil (Recommended)
    • Sunflower Oil

Method:

  1. Pat the steak dry with a paper towels to prevent unwanted moisture, a proper sear, and a proper crust on the steak.
  2. Season the steak with a generous amount of salt and pepper and make sure to rub and spread the seasoning evenly.
  3. Turn on the stoke with the highest heat and heat up the pan.
    • Make sure you have a stainless steel pan.
    • To make sure the pan is ready sprinkle some water on the pan and if it glides along the surface then the pan is ready.
  4. Add a samll amount of oil then lay one side of the steak flat on the pan and wait 1 min and 30 seconds.
    • Once it has been 1 minute and 30 seconds flip the steak and cook for the same amount of time on the other side
  5. After both sides have finished cooking, lower the heat and add the butter, garlic, and thyme and then baste each side for 30 seconds.
  6. Make sure to rest the steak for 5 minutes.
  7. After 5 minutes you are ready to serve the steak. Enjoy!
